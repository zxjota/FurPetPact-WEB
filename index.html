1 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

Welcome to FurPetPact! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our pet care management application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Note: FurPetPact is designed with your privacy in mind. We prioritize storing your information locally on your device whenever possible and minimize data collection to only what is necessary to provide our services.

2 Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register with the application or use its features, such as:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Email address
  • Profile information and preferences

Pet Information

To provide our pet care services, we collect and store information about your pets, such as:

  • Pet names, breeds, and birth dates
  • Medical history, vaccinations, and health records
  • Veterinary contact information
  • Dietary requirements and feeding schedules
  • Activity and exercise routines
  • Behavioral notes and training progress

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our application, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage statistics and feature interactions
  • Performance data and error reports (to help us improve the app)

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our pet care management services
  • To send you reminders for vaccinations, medications, and appointments
  • To personalize your pet care experience and provide relevant recommendations
  • To generate insights and reports about your pet's health and routines
  • To send you administrative information, such as updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities to enhance user experience
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

4 Data Storage and Local Processing

FurPetPact is designed to prioritize your privacy:

  • Local Storage: Your pet data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Optional Cloud Backup: Secure cloud backup is available as an optional feature, which you can enable or disable in settings
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect essential information needed to provide our services

5 Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations:

With Your Consent

We may disclose your information with your consent, such as when you choose to share pet health records with your veterinarian.

Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party vendors who perform services for us, such as:

  • Cloud storage providers (if you enable cloud backup)
  • Analytics services (in anonymized, aggregated form)
  • Customer support platforms

Emergency Situations

In emergency situations involving your pet's health and safety, we may share necessary information with veterinary services or emergency contacts you have designated.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information where required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6 Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures designed to protect the security of your personal and pet-related information. However, please remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.

7 Your Data Protection Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information in the app settings
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Export: You can export your pet data from the application

8 Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

9 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
